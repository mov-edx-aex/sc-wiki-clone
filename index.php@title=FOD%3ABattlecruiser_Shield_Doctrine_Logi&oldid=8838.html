"Logistics refers to ships and modules that provide remote support to other ships. In real life, the term "logistics" is typically used to describe the coordination of military troops and supplies, and EVE University has a Logistics Division that serves this role."
--E-Uni

If you think this might be your niche, then below you will find a few fits with training requirements for your progression in the logi role. Keep in mind that these are all shield rep fits; which is a much more challenging experience than the 'classic' armour rep. If you want to try logi to see if you like it without having to buy and fit your own ship, then the Osprey fit is the one to train for. If you find that you enjoy the role, then keep going and train for the ships after it!
