Exploded Explorer Ship Replacement Program (SRP)

Was your ship blasted to smithereens while exploring the far reaches of Anoikis? The Exploded Explorer SRP is here to help you get back into the ship of your choice and exploring the stars (or wormholes) again! See below for details.

Created and Managed by Mor Dovorot

Eligible Ships[edit]

T2 Covert Ops Frigates[edit]

  • Anathema
  • Buzzard
  • Cheetah
  • Helios

Sisters of EVE Frigate[edit]

  • Astero

Want to Participate? Follow These Simple Steps:[edit]

  1. Get your Anathema, Buzzard, Cheetah, Helios, or Astero blown up.
  2. Be a Signal Cartel member at the time the blowing up incident occurs.
  3. Be a Signal Cartel member at the time of the SRP request.
  4. Send Mor Dovorot an in-game personal mail requesting the replacement.
  5. (Optional) in your request include a hilarious and/or exaggerated story about what went on.
  6. Please specify where you want your new hull delivered:
    • Astero hulls can be delivered to Thera or Zoohen
    • Covert Ops hulls are only available in Zoohen (for now)
  7. IF it's an Anathema, Buzzard, Cheetah, or Helios, tell Paravindi Ruse thanks for giving Mor Dovorot the idea to expand the program to these hulls.
  8. Don't be shy and do the Step 4! If you don't make a request and Mor sees your killmail on ZKB, he'll assume that you're embarrassed about your loss (you shouldn't be) and will not attempt to lure you into participating in the program. 😉
  9. Fit the new hull and fly it like you got it for free (because you did)!

Thank You Note[edit]

Mor Dovorot would like to sincerely thank those who made contributions/donations to the SRP program - you know who you are, and he knows that you are awesome! The previous sentence is not a poorly masked solicitation for further contributions, as Mor can afford the program on his own for now; if anything, it keeps him playing EVE to be able to continually fund the program.
