The Directory of Important Information (DII) contains links for most if not all Signal Cartel corp policy and process questions.

Please Note: Questions are always welcome! For simple questions, ask in Alliance chat in-game (preferably) or talk with a recruiter; if necessary, the Leadership Team can be contacted here. For more involved discussions about admin and policy matters, post questions in our forums in a new thread under the appropriate category; the OP of such threads should link the relevant admin/policy.

REMINDER: Our forums, in-game channels, and comms (Discord) are FAMILY-FRIENDLY. All communications which contain vulgarity, excessive profanity, and other content (including content quoted from elsewhere) that moderators subjectively decide is not in keeping with that policy will be edited or deleted. If members see any communications which appear to be in violation of this policy, flag it and moderators will review and take action as necessary.

Signal Cartel Corp Life

New? Do These Things First:

  1. Review again the Orientation Guide - First 24 Hours
  2. Read the Orientation Guide - Mandatory Tasks and complete the tasks (within the stipulated timeframes)
  3. Read the Orientation Guide - First Week
  4. Read the Orientation Guide - First Month
  5. Read all items linked in the Corp Policies section below. Signal Cartel members are responsible to know this information. No excuses.

🔥 Corp Policies 🔥

Note: Knowing and adhering to corp policies is the responsibility of each Signal Cartel member. There will be no mercy for "I didn't know" as an excuse if members violate either the Credo or corp policies.
