The last section of the Orientation Guide provides useful information about Signal Cartel and also lists a number of activities, services, and tools that new pilots can utilize during their first month in the corporation and beyond.

We realize this is a lot to take in, so take your time, enjoy yourself, and note that there are no mandates upon your activities other than to be an active member of our corp. If you set some goals and fly safe (or dangerous), you'll eventually carve out your own niche within Signal Cartel.

As you will see, Signal Cartel provides a friendly, nurturing home that includes great benefits and opportunities for learning, fun, and profit. Let's get this section started and finish strong!

How Can I Get Involved In Corp Activities?[edit]

Signal Cartel has a robust variety of initiatives and programs for all Signaleers to try out and explore (see below). We strongly encourage you to get involved and find your own niche. This is an exploration-centric corp, so explore everything our corp has to offer as well as all of New Eden!

Leadership has also made an explicit point of not being corp content generators. Most of our now-established programs were created by Signaleers just like you. So if you have an idea for a corp activity not listed - Dream, Create, Do! 

Signal Cartel Mantra:
"Be the content you want to see..." - Mynxee, Signal Cartel co-founder and former CEO

For more advice on how to get involved in Signal Cartel, please click here.

In-Game Activities[edit]

Tend Wormhole Rescue Caches and Rescue Stranded Pilots with EvE-Scout Rescue (ESR)[edit]

EvE-Scout Rescue (ESR) is New Eden's premier wormhole rescue service and is one of the oldest divisions in Signal Cartel. ESR consists of two programs:

  • EvE-Scout Rescue Cache (ESRC)
    • Provides a basic emergency resource kit for capsuleers stranded in wormholes, regardless of alliance, sovereignty, or playstyle.
    • ESRC was previously discussed in the Orientation Guide - First Week.
  • Search and Rescue (SAR)
    • The SAR program is specifically organized to rescue capsuleers stranded in wormhole systems where there is no ESRC present, or in situations when a stranded capsuleer is unable to utilize the equipment in an existing rescue cache.

Scan and Map Thera and Turnur with Our EvE-Scout Mapping Division[edit]

  • The EvE-Scout Mapping Division operates as a crowdsourced service tasked with scanning and recording the current wormhole access locations into and out of the hub systems of Thera and Turnur for the benefit of all of New Eden. The wormholes are exclusively mapped by members of Signal Cartel. Our scouts are also compensated by the generous donations of capsuleers that find our service invaluable.
    • Additionally, Tekufah and Samari Arila Enaka, fellow Signaleers, will generously donate a fitted Covert Ops ship to you if you try out mapping for the first time! Click here for details!
  • Wait, What are Thera and Turnur?
    • According to Mynxee, Signal Cartel Co-founder, "Thera is a wormhole system akin to Grand Central station...on average, 20-some wormhole connections spawn there daily that go to locations all over New Eden: high-sec, low-sec, null-sec, and other wormholes. People--especially explorers, but also many PvP'ers--use Thera as a convenient shortcut to move quickly around New Eden." You may wish to visit Thera for use as a travel hub, to see the sights there, or to participate in EvE-Scout Mapping. Thera Scanning is the original project that lead to the creation of EvE-Scout Enclave and Signal Cartel!
    • Recently, the system of Turnur has become a nexus system much like Thera, except located in low-security space. However, this wasn't always the case. After an extreme stellar event devastated the system, a large number of wormhole connections spontaneously opened up for reasons unknown. This tragic event presented an opportunity for EvE-Scout to expand our mapping services to Turnur.

Track Dangerous Storms Across New Eden with Our Signal Cartel Storm Tracking Service[edit]

Nullsec is currently experiencing wandering storms that slowly travel between systems called Metaliminal Storms. These weather events produce a variety of environmental effects that impact all ships brave enough to enter them. Help us report the stormy weather for the benefit of all capsuleers!

  • Click here to learn how to track storms with us!

Participate in Fleets* and Events[edit]

Fleets[edit]
  • The Fleet Ops Division (FOD) is a group of Signaleers with varying levels of experience in commanding, supporting, and participating in fleet operations; the goal is for all of us to improve our skills, build confidence, “try on” new roles in a safe environment and have some fun with our corpmates along the way. Participating in Fleets is also a terrific way to meet your fellow Signaleers, create lasting bonds and shared memories! Remember, Signal Cartel fleets are PvE only.
    • Check out the Discord Events Calendar (at the top of the channel list) and our 🗪ready-room forum channel for a list of upcoming Fleets!
    • Have questions about our FOD? We have answers at the #fod-general Discord channel. Swing by and say hi!

*Disclaimer: Fireworks and Medical Clones may be Activated! 💥 YAY!

How Do I Get Prepared for My First Fleet?[edit]
  1. Sign up for the fleet (to let us know you're coming!)
  2. Find out the Fleet Doctrine (this will be listed in the Fleet Op Announcement) and then pick which ship you will be flying in the fleet.
  3. If needed, train the necessary skills to fly your chosen ship.
    1. Click here for instructions on accessing our in-game FOD Doctrine Training Plans
  4. Fit your ship using our in-game Signal Cartel Corp Fittings for your particular Doctrine. Instructions are available here.
  5. Read up on FOD and various Fleet mechanics here.
  6. Always ask questions, we're a friendly and helpful bunch!
Events[edit]
  • Signal Cartel also holds various non-fleet Events throughout the year. These can range from our weekly informal Coffee Talks, annual CrinkleFest Rescue Cache Event, Fireside Chats with Leadership, Secret Santas, or Cyno Vigils.
    • Please visit the Discord Events Calendar (at the top of the channel list) and also our 🗪event-planning forum channel for more information.
Create and Organize Your Own Fleet or Event![edit]

If you don't see any fleets or events that strike your fancy, we have a solution: take the reins, point us in the right direction, and lead us on an adventure!
Protip: Organizing a Hugs Fleet is a terrific low-pressure way to get your fleet feet wet!

  • Fleets:
    • Please post your fleet in the 🗪ready-room forum channel by using the Fleet Announcement Template.
  • Non-Fleet Events:
    • Please post your event in the 🗪event-planning forum channel

NOTE: In either case, don't forget to promote it so people show up (click here for some tips).

Tour the Wonders of New Eden and Share Your Experiences with Signal Cartel Expedition TripTiks[edit]

Signal Cartel Expedition TripTiks provide a list of interesting beacons, landmarks, or other sites of interest in New Eden that would make for a fun expedition either solo or in a group. Each TripTik provides summarized info for each site and links to more in-depth external resources.

  • Click here for more info or to get involved.
Special Note: Make sure your list includes a trip to the Journey of Katia Sae Memorial!

Love Exploration but Still Want to Do Some Space Trucking or Industry on the Side? We have You Covered.[edit]

While hauling with your Signal Cartel character is not advised due to frequent wardecs, there is an alternative. Crikey Freight is a hauling and industry corp set up by Signal Cartel members. All members are the out-of-corp alts of Signaleers, and they are immune to wardecs.

  • Crikey Freight Wants You(r Alt)!

Work Towards Joining the Anoikis Division (AD) and LIVE in a Wormhole![edit]

Jumping through your first wormhole can be a scary proposition. Exploring the unknown of J-space can be even scarier. Permanently living there?! You must be crazy! Well, not for our brave members of the Anoikis Division (AD) that elevate wormhole living to an art form. Living in a wormhole provides its own unique set of challenges - constantly changing ingress/egress routes, sporadic access to trade hubs and known-space jump clones, and constant danger from PvP-focused pilots and corps. The rewards, other than ISK, are vast - thrills, excitement, camaraderie, and just plain fun! It's Wormhole Living at its Finest!

Here is a partial list of activities that AD members can pursue:

  • The explorer’s mainstay of scanning and hacking of data/relic sites.
  • PvE Combat: Sleeper sites and other NPCs in our wormhole, as well as other systems accessed through connecting wormholes.
  • Planetary Interaction (PI): A good way to generate a passive income. Our wormhole has several planets and can produce, up to and including, P4 products.
  • Mining: Random spawns of ore, ice fields and gas clouds within and outside of our wormhole. We also have a moondrill for extraction of moon ore for mining.
  • Industrial: Manufacturing and reprocessing. Other industrial services, such as Invention, Reactions, are available.
  • Participation in the Corp’s Search and Rescue (SAR) and EvE-Scout Rescue Cache (ESRC) programs.

If interested, Signaleers must meet the AD's eligibility requirements. Therefore, while you can't join the AD within your first month, you can start your journey towards living the wormhole life now! Click here for more info on the AD.

Out-of-Game Activities[edit]

Complete Fun Achievements and Join In-Corp “Clubs”[edit]

Check out all the cool things we've come up with! What else do you have to do, right? 😉

Even if you aren't an "achievement unlocked" type of gamer, please at the very least consider posting your First Signal Cartel Service Activity on our Discord server, so that we can all give you a big pat on the back!

Have an Artistic Eye and Interested in Astrophotography? Visit Our Observatory[edit]

The EvE-Scout Observatory is a gathering place for fellow New Eden Astrophotographers to inspire and be inspired by the majesty of New Eden. Please post your favorite images directly to our #eve-observatory channel on Discord. The Observatory looks forward to seeing your submissions!

Be an Active Signaleer on Discord[edit]

Signal Cartel is a tight-knit community and we are here for you. Have questions? Ask! Have knowledge to share? Share it! Have something funny to say? Say it! Lost your ship to a gatecamp? Tell us your war story, but remember - We have a NO TEARS Policy! Help us serve New Eden and maximize your fun!

  • Join us on our EvE-Scout Enclave Discord Server!

Earn Your Signal Cartel Academy Diploma* by Requesting/Attending Another Class[edit]

The Signal Cartel Academy (SCA) was introduced in the previous section of the Orientation Guide - First Week.

  • You already attended your first class, right?
  • Review the previous Orientation Guide - First Week if you "forgot" or if you're thirsty for more! Remember, learning means you're still trying.
  • However, the SCA isn't meant to be a one-way street of knowledge given from on high. As Xalyar, the founder of SCA has stated, "Our goal is to provide the needed tools to facilitate the meeting of mentors and students, where anyone can volunteer their time and knowledge - thus becoming a mentor - for the benefit of anyone - thus becoming a student - who feels they need unfulfilled guidance." So scratch that teaching itch by offering up your expertise for the benefit of all Signaleers!

*Not a thing

Join the EVE Echoes Chapter of Signal Cartel[edit]

EVE Echoes is a standalone mobile game developed by NetEase Games and based on the EVE Online universe. Signal Cartel and the Credo are now mobile! Note that corp participation in EVE Echoes counts towards your overall activity requirement to be considered a Signal Cartel member in good standing.

  • For more information on joining us from the comfort of your couch, please click here.

Create Amazing or Amazingly Wacky Ship Fits with Splunkworks[edit]

Splunkworks is Signal Cartel's not so clandestine, ship research and development division. If you are a ship fitting guru or, on the contrary, are completely lost and have questions about fitting your first exploration frigate, this is the place for you! Share your knowledge with us or refine your fits in the #fitting-lab Discord channel.

  • Click here to learn how to format fits for posting on Discord.
  • Protip: Check out Pyfa, THE ship fitting tool for EVE

Consider Becoming a Wormhole Researcher[edit]

The EvE-Scout Wormhole Research Center's purpose is to gather and verify wormhole information with the goal of forming our own static wormhole data collection. Ultimately, this may one day result in a cool new service to New Eden!

  • Visit the #research-center channel on the EvE-Scout Enclave Discord server to participate and learn more.

Explore, Read, and Contribute to Our Corp Wiki[edit]

The stated goal of The Explorer's Compendium, our corp wiki, is for it to become the best exploration resource in the Universe - where the combined knowledge of Signal Cartel is collaboratively documented, categorized, and preserved for the benefit of all corp members. As the BEST exploration corp in New Eden, we are surely up to the task, but we need your help. Contribute today!

  • A great way to get your feet wet is to participate in our Exploration Adopt-A-Page Initiative!
  • Take a stab at filling out a stub page that needs some love.
  • Please read our Wiki Editing Guidelines before getting started.
  • Any questions, please reach out to Vega Blazar on Discord.

Services Available to Corp Members[edit]

EVE is hard. Playing EVE alone is even harder. In Signal Cartel, in addition to helpful discourse, the corp offers a range of services to aid all our members. Please take advantage of these opportunities - it's why they're offered!

  • Click here to learn more.

Useful Corp Tools[edit]

Signal Cartel uses various out-of-game tools and programs to assist in our exploration efforts. Utilizing some or all of these tools will help make the game more enjoyable by increasing your situational awareness, productivity, and ability to serve New Eden.

  • Click here to learn more.

AFK Thread[edit]

You just joined, so why go AFK? Real life happens though, so if you plan to be away from the game for less than 60 days please let us know by making a post in the 🗪hello-farewell-afk Discord forum channel. Be sure to use the "AFK" tag when you post. It's not necessary but is nice and will keep us from wondering what happened to you!

Signal Cartel Swag Store[edit]

The Signal Cartel Swag Store has a twofold purpose: to distribute awesome merchandise to our awesome members and to help us cover the real-life overhead for all our awesome services. While infrastructure costs are not incredibly high, management appreciates any and all help; after all, ISK won't pay for everything! To this end, please note that all store profits will be used to support corp services. Represent your new corp in style and help us keep the RL lights on!

Conclusion[edit]

Whew! You made it! Congratulations! We hope the Signal Cartel New Member Orientation Guide has been helpful to you. Now the real adventure begins. If you haven't already, undock that ship and start exploring the wonders of New Eden. o7
