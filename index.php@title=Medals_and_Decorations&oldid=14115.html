  • The Signal Cartel - EvE-Scout Distinguished Service Medal

Angel Signaleer For generously contributing exemplary material or logistics support to benefit our members or programs.
Big Damn Hero For a noble sacrifice on behalf of another.
Heart of Quinn Awarded to Signal Cartel members who over time demonstrate a combination of remarkable generosity toward, concern for, and mentoring of new players as exemplified by Signaleer Quinn Valerii, for whom this medal is named.
Lovable Grinch Award There's one in every crowd!
Marshmallow Curmudgeon Awarded to cranky, grumbling bittervets who still care enough to launch and oversee initiatives that benefit new players in particular and the corp as a whole.
Nerves of Steel Awarded to Signal Cartel members who commit brazen acts of courage in the face of overwhelming danger.
Quantum Signal Booster For extraordinary videos, guides, tools, and other creations that boost our presence and reputation in the New Eden community.
Shepherd Signaleer For demonstrating consistent effort to share expertise and mentor others, especially new players.
Signal Booster Awarded for special and/or creative initiatives to promote Signal Cartel in-game.
Signal Cartographer Awarded to those who continually push the borders of and document the frontier, for the benefit and enlightenment of New Eden as a whole.
Signal Crest of Friendship For actions exemplifing our ideals of friendship.
Signal Hot Shot Awarded for showing exemplary initiative in support of corp programs within the first 30 days of membership.
Signal Igniter Awarded to Signaleers whose conduct inspires materially positive responses from others in New Eden.
Signal Path Lighter Awarded to those who organize memorial cyno and traditional fleets for notable capsuleers and others, to help light their way onward.
Signal Philosopher For spreading love, happiness, and credo cheer among the troops.
Signal Pinch-Hitter For cheerfully stepping in to fill the shoes of a benched comrade.
Signal Ringmaster Awarded to recognize Signal Cartel members who organize and emcee special events in New Eden such as treasure hunts, races, competitions, or community service activities for the corp or the wider community of capsuleers.
Signal Services Offsite Operator Awarded to a Signaleer whose call of duty exceeds game boundaries and who strives to assist through any means available despite being unable to log in.
Signal Shared Storyteller Awarded for taking part in the group storytelling fun and making New Eden come to life in whole new ways.
Signal Solver Awarded to pilots who on their own initiative solve problems or leverage opportunities in ways that benefit the corp as a whole.
Signal Star Creator Awarded for taking the initiative in dreaming up and executing terrific content. "Be the content you want to see in Signal Cartel."
Signal Star of Honor Awarded to Signaleers for actions demonstrating extraordinary integrity.
Sirius Hero For demonstrating stellar performance and dedication on behalf of EvE-Scout.
Stellar Evolution For displaying marked progress in developing knowledge of the game, skill capabilities, and confidence. This pilot knows how to fly sharp!
The Sagan Star of Distinction Awarded for extraordinary accomplishments which demonstrate the true spirit of exploration.
The Signal Cartel Awarded to those who exemplify the spirit of service that is at the heart of Signal Cartel and EvE-Scout, both in support of our service programs and in guidance and mentoring of corp members.

SAR Silver Lifesaver Medal Awarded to a Signaleer who has gone above and beyond the call of duty to save the lives of 10 or more Capsuleers stranded in Anoikis.
SAR Gold Lifesaver Medal Awarded to a Signaleer who has gone above and beyond the call of duty to save the lives of 50 or more Capsuleers stranded in Anoikis.
Beacon of Anoikis Awarded to pilots upon completing 100 successful Search and Rescue operations.

Scout of the Far Horizon Awarded to Signal Cartel members that have traversed the entirety of Anoikis tending or sowing rescue caches in each system as a service to the community of New Eden.

Triple Crown of the Cluster Awarded to Signaleers who achieve the arduous yet prestigious Triple Crown by simultaneously being the Top Rescue Cacher, Top Thera Scanner, and Top Storm Chaser within the same 24-hour period.
VPF Distinguished Liberation Medal Awarded for valor and courage in times of need. The pilot displayed exceptional discipline and gallantry during the liberation of J005223.
