Following is the basics fitting we look for in our PvE Armor Battlecruiser Doctrines. We have 3 dps ships available, and 2 different fittings. Tier 1 of each ship is the alpha friendly version. After testing on a new character, you can fly one of these in a little over a week (depending on character faction)

The Tier 1 fits and relevant skills needed to train are:

Conclusion[edit]

These are the minimum fits we allow on our Armour fleets. They are tried and tested and can survive what a c3 or c6 throws at it. If you are interested in flying one of these in our fleets, feel free to skill up. We have loaner ships available for you to use, you just need those minimum skills to fly them!

Feel free to add in whatever you; like/need, in respect to the tier of the module: Tier1, Meta or Tier 2 mods will be covered by SRP, Faction/Deadspace modules will not

If you do not know what to add, hit Null Flare up and we will discuss what works best for you.


Please direct any questions or concerns to Katherine Skysong or Xavec using in-game mail, discord or the forums.
