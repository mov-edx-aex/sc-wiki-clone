  • HY-RWO
TIP:
Set each system in the TripTik as a waypoint, then choose Autopilot options to establish a route for your expedition.
TRAVEL NOTE:
HY-RWO is deep in 0.0 space, involving jumps across a number of different alliance territories, many of which may be operating under “Not Blue, Shoot It” protocol. Gate camps (including warp interdiction bubbles) can be found often in transitioning from 0.0 to high security space, as well as on other gates. Caution is advised. Warp to the Beacon when in System.


TripTik055 Expedition Highlights

In the heart of Catch, a select few Amarrian battleships, encircled by a swarm of agile frigates, lie in anticipation, poised for a decisive strike.

Introduction

An agile and swift Imperial force, led by a renowned commander celebrated for commanding a personalized Bestower, stands ready for swift and decisive actions.

Locations

  • HY-RWO

In the HY-RWO system, specifically around planet 3 and its first moon, a group consisting of five Imperial Templar Ultara battleship-class ships and multiple Divine Imperial Imran/Napat frigate-class vessels maintains a precise orbit around a compact and mobile lookout tower. At a distance, the force's Commander, seated in his Bestower, oversees the activities of his men and women. Everyone in the force, excluding the general, is identified by the color red, symbolizing their allegiance to The Empire. General Karo Zulak holds the rank of a level 4 Amarr COSMOS Agent.
