Membership Medals[edit]

Signaleers do some pretty amazing things on a daily basis. We bet you've seen plenty of them and that's worth sharing! Therefore, if you think a fellow Signaleer deserves to be especially recognized, why not nominate them for a Membership Medal listed below? These medals are open to all Signal Cartel members for nomination.

Once you've chosen the appropriate medal, follow this link for more information on How to Nominate Members for Medals.
