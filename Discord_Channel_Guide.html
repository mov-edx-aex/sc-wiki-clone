This is a summary of the channels available on our EvE-Scout Enclave Discord Server.

Where it is not said otherwise specifically (hint: PUBLIC), the channels are visible only to the Signal Cartel members (aka Signaleers).

On Discord, you can see a short description for each channel. Click on the channel, and you will see the summary on the top of the Discord page directly by the channel name. Click on the description itself, and the whole description will be visible in a popup window.

Channel name Description
Event Event calendar for Signal Cartel members.
Browse Channels List of all accessible channels plus keyword search
----------------------- ---------------------------------
#welcome Welcome channel for those new to the server.
rules Our Discord server rules.
public-announcements PUBLIC announcements channel meant for the greater New Eden community.
#explo-lounge PUBLIC chat for friends of SC.
#ccp-news EVE Online and CCP news feed.
#links-resources Links and resources related to Signal Cartel.
SIGNALEERS Category
#announcements Internal announcements and important messages/pings by Leadership and Division Managers.
hello-farewell-afk New Signaleers introduce themselves here. Seasoned Signaleers notify the rest about temporary leave of EVE, or permanent leave of SC.
#members-lounge General chat for the Signaleers.
#fitting-lab Discussion about theory, crafting, and fitting ships with our Splunkworks Division.
#sc-wiki Discussion, feedback, and suggestions regarding The Explorer's Compendium, our wiki, located here.
#eve-echoes Discussion centered around EVE Echoes, a standalone mobile game developed by NetEase Games.
#industry-market Chat about EVE industry and market.
#rp-in-character Chat channel dedicated to character roleplaying.
#sc-support-services This channel is for all your Quartermaster, SCRAMS, SCRUBS, and Crikey Freight needs/questions/discussions. A list of all our Corp Services is available here.
explo-voice Voice channel Signaleer only.
FORUMS Category
event-planning Channel to plan and promote non-fleet events.
gameplay-discussions Discuss specific aspects of EVE play (scanning, hacking, exploration, implants, etc.)
credo-culture-discussions Discussion forum channel about the Credo and our corporation culture.
achievements-challenges-clubs List of all our achievements, challenges, and clubs. Take a look and show us what you got!
1420-expeditionary Channel about creating new TripTiks.
#old-forums-support Get help resolving issues with our old EvE-Scout Forums (https://forums.eve-scout.com/). These forums are now in read-only maintenance mode.
out-of-game-discussions Forum channel about out-of-game topics.
engineering Chat about ideas around creating and improving the suite of tools the Signaleers use as we explore New Eden and Anoikis. Check out our Engineering Division here.
SC ACADEMY Category
education-need-offer Ask here if you need special training in some SC activities (e.g. tending caches). Offer training on a topic you know a lot about and can be helpful to the other Signaleers. Read our Session Posting Guidelines for Discord for more info.
#sca-campus Share your knowledge and skills and learn from others! Mainly used for chat, comments and questions during ⁠sca-auditorium talks/presentations. Read more about the Signal Cartel Academy (SCA) here.
sca-auditorium Voice and video channel for training and presentations.
FLEETS Category
ready-room Chat here when gathering for a fleet event. Sign up for upcoming fleets and view recent after action reports (AARs).
#fod-general Discussion about Fleet Operations Division (FOD) policies, activities, and organization.
FLEET 1 (EUR) Voice channel during a fleet event organized for players in the European time zones.
FLEET 2 (EUR) Voice channel during a fleet event organized for players in the European time zones.
FLEET 3 (USA) Voice channel during a fleet event organized for players in the USA time zones.
FLEET 4 (USA) Voice channel during a fleet event organized for players in the USA time zones.
FLEET 5 (AUS) Voice channel during a fleet event organized for players in the Australian time zones.
EVE-SCOUT RESCUE Category
#stranded-pilots-lounge PUBLIC chat for stranded pilots.
#esr-longue Chat about tending caches and other EVE-Scout Rescue (ESR) topics.
rescue-reports Weekly SAR and ESRC rescue reports.
EVE-SCOUT MAPPING Category
#mapping-lounge Discussion and coordination for the EvE-Scout Thera and Turnur Scanning.
#research-center Chat dedicated to the EVE-Scout Wormhole Research Center (ESWRC).
PUBLIC CHANNELS Category
#public-eve-questions PUBLIC channel for EVE questions.
#eve-observatory PUBLIC pictures and screenshots created while playing the game.
#eve-lore-and-backstory PUBLIC chat about EVE lore and storylines.
#developers PUBLIC chat about coding projects, computers, and other tech-related pursuits.
PUBLIC-COMMS PUBLIC voice channel for real time information sharing.
MEETUPS Category
#rl-meetup-planning PUBLIC chat for pilots to more easily coordinate plans and schedules for meeting up at official CCP events or for more informal ones.
rl-meetup-planning PUBLIC voice channel for real time meetup planning.
