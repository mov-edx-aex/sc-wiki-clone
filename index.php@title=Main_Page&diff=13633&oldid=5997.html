The aim of the Academy is to lay out a simple and efficient framework that enables access to the wealth of knowledge, skills, and expertise found within Signal Cartel through the exchange of information between Signaleers. Buddies are a friendly point of contact that provide help and guidance as new members adjust to life as a Signaleer.

Splunkworks (SW)[edit]

Splunkworks, named as a homage to the famous Lockheed Skunkworks and to Signal Cartel co-founder Johnny Splunk, is the division of Signal Cartel that advises members on selecting, fitting, and flying the best ship for their needs. Regular offerings include updating and revising the Corp Fits, instituting fun ‚ÄúFitting Challenge‚Äù contests, and providing more detailed guides for common Signal Cartel use cases, such as Covops Exploration and Wormhole PvE. In addition, our Splunkworks group is our go-to consultant for Fleet Doctrine concept planning.

Thera and Turnur Scanning (TS)[edit]

The EvE-Scout Mapping Division documents the ever-changing wormhole connections into and out of the hub systems of Thera and Turnur. This free service is provided to the greater New Eden community for the benefit of all capsuleers regardless of playstyle or allegiance. Our scouts are compensated by the generous donations of those that find our service invaluable.

Wiki Division[edit]

The Wiki Division knows the answer to the ultimate question of life, the universe, and everything. Hint: it's not 42, well...maybe. Seriously though, the wiki is the knowledge vault of Signal Cartel, housing all our important corp documents and information, from the Credo to our excellent Expedition TripTiks. Since you're already here, why not take a look around? For members, By members, For you.
