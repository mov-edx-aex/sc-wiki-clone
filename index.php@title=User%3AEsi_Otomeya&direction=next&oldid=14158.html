Entering a System

The the moment I "pop" a wormhole (WH), I do the following:

  1. Bookmark the wormhole I came through as "< Wormhole" (see below)
  2. Find a planet or moon as many AU away as I can get
  3. I warp to the moon or planet, but I get no closer than 50km, BUT...
  4. I also activate my cloak after I initiate warp, AND I launch my probes
  5. I load up the system map and I move my probes off the system grid and then press "B" (so they warp and scan)
  6. I then load up the shared bookmarks and find the cache
  7. I warp to the cache 20-30km away
  8. If it's safe, I tend or sow it
  9. I then fly away again, to a moon/planet, and bounce around to make a safe spot
    1. I bookmark the safe spot "! SAFE SPOT 001"
  10. I now reload the system map and move my probes as needed, scanning down WHs and sites

Bookmarks

When entering a system, I begin scanning for stuff... obviously. When I find it, I bookmark it like everyone, but I have a few ideas around _how_ I bookmark the things I find:

  • "< Wormhole": the wormhole I came through to get to this system, so `<` basically meaning "the way back"
  • "> ": the wormhole I went through when leaving the system. I use this for when/if I come back, so I know where I just was... yeah...
  • "? ": a wormhole with an unknown destination or some other "thing" I don't know about
  • "! ": a safe spot. The use of "!" moves the item to the top of the list
  • "- ": a site I cannot tank/DPS because the NPCs are too tough (C4+ sites)
  • "+ ": a site I can take on (hack), and I will eventually
  • "$ ": a successfully farmed site
