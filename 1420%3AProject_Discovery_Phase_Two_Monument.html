TIP:
Set each system in the TripTik as a waypoint, then choose Autopilot options to establish a route for your expedition.
TRAVEL NOTE:
This site is located in 0.8 high security space that is owned by concord. If your security status is -3.0 or -5 with Concord you can encounter issues with the faction police. Warp to the Beacon when in System.

TripTik053 Expedition Highlights

Check out the monument for the second phase of project discovery.

Introduction

See the monument dedicated to capsuleers who analyzed real world astronomical data.

Locations

  • Pakhshi

In the year YC119 (2017) project discovery moved from the Sisters of Eve to the CONCORD Assembly. The project changed to having capsuleers analyze data to discover new real world exoplanets. For the site itself make sure to observe the hologram showing a solar system representing the research of the capsuleers and the 5 satellites that surround the site.
