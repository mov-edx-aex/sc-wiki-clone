Think you can't participate in a fleet event because you have little (or no) experience? Think again Signaleer! You can learn by doing, and we're here to help. This thread collects the AARs from FOD operations to show you it can be done. Take inspiration from these stories and jump in to an upcoming fleet! Better yet, organize one of your own! Don't know where to start? Reach out to us, and we can help you put it together!

2020 After Action Reports[edit]

  • Abyssal Deadspace #6 - It has finally happened! T3 has been conquered!
  • A-BC C5 Combat Fleet - Another interesting fleet, a lot of snap decisions from everyone involved and only a few losses overall. Good stuff!
  • World War Chappy - An interesting where Signal Cartel paid their respects during a capsuleer's last birthday.
  • Abyssal Deadspace #5 - Another good round of victories in T1 and T2 abyssals!
  • A-BC C5 Combat Fleet - Null Flare leads our people in his first ever C5 combat fleet!
  • Helping Allison: part deux! - Katherine leads another squad of Signaleers to assist ARC and keep on looking for a cure for Allison.
  • A-BC C3 Combat Fleet - Another solid outing in jspace, where even DDOS and instability couldn't stop us!
  • Helping Allison - A few brave Signaleers join in on a joint operation with ARC in order to help find a cure for Allison's ailments!
  • Abyssal Deadspace Training Grounds #4 - Another Abyssal fleet we know and love!
  • Trig Trail Run - The first ever outing of the SCFS against the Triglavian menace!
  • How Do I Do This Again? - A short-lived PvE outing interrupted by neighbouring wormholers! We fought well!
  • SCFS Abyssal Deadspace Training Grounds #3 - Our third dive into the Abyss that saw us rise from the dead after the slaughter that was our last outing to turn it into glorious victory over the second tier of Abyssal Deadspace!
  • Headed for Mount Wannahockaloogie - A very nice PvE operation in a C4 Wolf-Rayet crackful of combat sites!
  • SCFS Abyssal Deadspace Training Grounds #2 - Our second jump in the Abyss; the iron price was paid, blood was shed but many lessons were learned!
  • SCFS Abyssal Deadspace Training Grounds - The first time the SCFS had a crack at the Abyssal Deadspace content!
  • SCFS A-BC Holiday Fleet - A PvE outing interrupted by hostilities but where Signal Cartel prevailed!
  • SCFS A-BC C3 Sleeper Eradication - The first ever PvE outing of the new SCFS team; and what a ride! Learn all about @Katherine-Skysong first time as a Fleet Commander!
  • SCA Beginner's Exploration Fleet II - A swell exploration lead by our very own @Katherine-Skysong, showing new explorers the ropes!
  • Let's give this a shot - The fleet in-between administrations; also the first ever fleet with @Null-Flare as Fleet Commander!
