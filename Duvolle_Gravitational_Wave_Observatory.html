TripTik037 Expedition Highlights

Visit a System where you can view the Trace Cosmos.

Introduction

Have you ever heard of a phenomenon called "Trace Cosmos"? It is a peculiar purple-haze-colored vaporous cloud that can be seen with the naked eye from the Gererique High Sec system. This field's genesis is attributed to a cluster of minor black holes and atypical spatial rifts by scientists. The Duvolle Laboratories corporate research facility is located near this strange cloud. The Shadow Cartel Alliance's Astrahus guards the system.
