Welcome to The Splunkworks Division!

Signal Cartel's not so clandestine, ship research and development division for all your ship, fitting, and theorycrafting needs.

This is the place where Theorycrafters like to hang out and tell stories about 'The one that got away', or maybe, 'That time that we got away'. But as much as we all like fireside chats, there is work to be done.

With the fairly steady supply of fresh capsuleers that New Eden provides us, there are nearly endless opportunities to help out our fellow Signaleers! We all know how steep the EVE Learning Curve is! It is one thing to give someone a link to a complete fit in alliance chat; it is quite a different experience to teach them what it means, and how it all works together.

In addition, EVE is ever-changing - new events pop up, slot layouts change, nerfs happen. With all these things in mind, there is a solid chance that one's information is, unbeknownst to them, outdated. So the more ears we have to the ground, the better.

The Splunkworks Division aims to be a place where Signaleers come to gain knowledge, as well as share it.

In order to get help or help others with their ships and fittings, Splunkworks utilizes two main methods of communication:

  • Splunkworks forum
  • #fitting-lab Discord channel

Exploration Ships

Choosing an exploration ship is a personal choice filled with pros and cons. The Exploration Ship Guide is meant to help make that decision easier. Note that this guide is a WIP.

Exploration Ship Fitting Guide

Once you have decided on a ship to fly, you will need to think about how to equip it for exploring New Eden. The Exploration Ship Fitting Guide discusses the key equipment used for exploring - the rest of the fit will depend on the slots available (and your fitting skills).

Official Signal Cartel Corp Fittings

Don't forget to check out the official Signal Cartel Corp Fittings for various ships!

  1. Open the in-game Fitting window (Alt-F)
  2. Click on the Equipment tab
  3. Click on the Hulls & Fits button
  4. Click the Filter: Corporation Fittings button
  5. Browse our various corp ship fittings or search for your desired ship
  6. Fit your ship
  7. Fly dangerously and enjoy!

How to Get Involved with Splunkworks

For Those Starting Out:

  • Get help in figuring out how to fit ships.
  • Learn why we fit ships in certain ways.
  • Learn more about which modules do what, and why.
  • Get help in understanding the strengths and weaknesses of different hulls.

For Those With Know-How to Spare:

  • Help in teaching all of the above.
  • Show newer pilots other possibilities they may not have thought of.
  • Collaborate with other theorycrafters and share your ideas and fits.
  • Help organize instructional and entertaining in-game operations.
  • Help design and test fits for upcoming event sites.

Future Splunkworks (not so secret) Projects

  • “Fit of the Month”
  • A monthly forum post from the Splunkworks Team
  • Chances to collaborate with us in the testing of new fits
  • Opportunities to test your own theories and fits
  • And even recurring in-game events designed to hone your skills as an explorer, prizes included, who doesn't like prizes!
