Background[edit]

The Signal Cartel Swag Store has a twofold purpose:

  1. To distribute awesome swag to our awesome members.
  2. To help us cover the IT overhead for all our awesome services.

While infrastructure costs are not incredibly high, management appreciates any and all help; after all, ISK won't pay for everything! To this end, please note that all store profits will be used to support corp services such as:

  • The Explorer's Compendium (Signal Cartel Wiki)
  • The EvE-Scout Rescue website
  • The Signal Cartel website
  • Other fun corporate stuff (contest prizes, shipping on physical gifts, etc.)

How Do I Order?[edit]

  1. Go to our Signal Cartel Swag Store page on Zazzle.
  2. Place your order.
  3. Party! ðŸ¥³

Important: While there are other products on Zazzle with our logos, these are from an old store that never launched. Do not buy these because Signal Cartel will not receive the proceeds. Only purchase items from the "Signal Cartel Swag Store" directly.

Who Do I Contact with Questions?[edit]

  • Katia Sae, Signal Cartel Director and Alliance Executor, is the point of contact for all Swag Store orders (effective March 2021).
    • Please send a Discord DM with any questions.
    • If there is a product with a particular alliance brand asset you'd like to see, please contact Katia to get the item set up through Zazzle.

Quarterly Report[edit]

In an effort to provide complete transparency about all the real life income and expenses related to the Swag Store, Leadership will publish and update a report at least once each quarter. If needed, please reach out to Katia Sae with any questions via Discord.

The Quarterly Zazzle Signal Cartel Swag Store Income and Expenses Report is available here.
