Overview[edit]

A Skill Plan is an ordered list of skill levels with specific goals, which you can create, share, and keep track of to follow your training within the game client. With skill plans, a player is able to build a list of skills without needing to buy and inject them first.

Corporation Skill Plans work much the same way except these skill plans are managed by players with CEO, Director, or Skill Plan Manager roles. Players gain access to Corporation Skill Plans once they join a player-owned corporation like Signal Cartel.

Corporation Plans are divided into four categories.

  • Explorer
  • Enforcer
  • Industrialist
  • Soldier of Fortune

Signal Cartel Corporation Skill Plans[edit]

As you might expect, Signal Cartel's Corporation Skill Plans are heavily geared towards exploration. Therefore, a great starting point is the "Explorer's 16" Skill Plan and the aptly named "Scanning" Skill Plan both found under the "Explorer" category. However, there are other great Skill Plans available, so please browse the other categories to find a plan that matches your current goals.

Protip: Hover your mouse over a specific Skill Plan for a detailed description of that particular plan.

Fleet Ops Division (FOD) Doctrine Training Plans[edit]

  • Of note, Signal Cartel's FOD houses their Fleet Doctrine Training Plans under the "Soldier of Fortune" category. Check them out, skill up, and get ready for the next fleet!
  • The Fleet Doctrine Training Plans are also available in an EVEMon compatible format.
  • Feel free to reach out to Katherine Skysong, Xavec, or Null Flare if you have questions regarding FOD.

How to Access Our Corp Skill Plans[edit]

  1. While in-game, click the "Skills" icon on the Neocom panel to make the "Skills" window appear.
  2. At the top of the "Skills" window, click "Skill Plans" and then "Corporation Plans".
  3. Click on the particular category icon that interests you.
  4. Browse the various Skill Plans.
    • If you are missing the required skillbooks for a particular Skill Plan, you will be notified.
  5. Start training! :)
