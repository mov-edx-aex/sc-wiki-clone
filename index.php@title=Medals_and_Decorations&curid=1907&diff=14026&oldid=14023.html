Fleet Operations Division (FOD)

  • Accidental Explorer Award

  • Fleet School Graduate Ribbon

  • FOD Medal of Cooperation

  • FOD Medal of Leadership

  • FOD Medal of Scouting

  • Signal Fleet Fanatic

Accidental Explorer Award - Awarded to navigationally-challenged FCs who lead their fleets to unexpected places and unanticipated adventures!
Fleet School Graduate Ribbon - Awarded to Signaleers who step into a new role and serve for the first time as Fleet Commander or Logi Pilot in a Signal Cartel Fleet School fleet
FOD Medal of Cooperation - Awarded to Signaleers willing to tackle New Eden's most challenging content.
FOD Medal of Leadership - Awarded to Signaleers willing to carry the heavy responsibility of fleet leadership on their shoulders
FOD Medal of Scouting - Awarded to Signaleers willing to light a way for their fleet and protect them from the shadows
Signal Fleet Fanatic - Awarded for consistent efforts to organize, FC, and/or participate in fleet ops with other members.

Signal Cartel Wiki and TripTiks

  • Signal Amplifier

Signal Amplifier - For noteworthy contributions to Signal Cartel's internal knowledge documentation and wiki.

Signal Cartel TripTiks

  • TripTik Researcher

  • TripTik Signaleer

  • TripTik Historian

TripTik Researcher - Awarded in recognition of contributions to Signal Cartel's TripTik Service for having completed 1 TripTik.
TripTik Signaleer - Awarded in recognition of contributions to Signal Cartel's TripTik Service for having completed 5 TripTiks.
TripTik Historian - Awarded in recognition of contributions to Signal Cartel's TripTik Service for having completed 10+ TripTiks.

Retired Medals

General Membership (retired)

  • 1000th Member

  • Bold Expeditioneer

  • Signal Snipe Sacrificer

  • The 'Edo' Special Snowflake Trinket

  • Wayward Explorer

1000th Member - Awarded to the pilot who had the good fortune to be our 1000th member.
Bold Expeditioneer - Awarded to pilots upon completing their first three group expeditions to tour famous landmarks in New Eden.
Signal Snipe Sacrificer - Awarded for refraining from stealing key membership milestones with alts.
The 'Edo' Special Snowflake Trinket - Awarded to medal-obsessed members for completing trivial yet amusing tasks suggested by Signal Cartel leadership.
Wayward Explorer - Awarded to Signaleers who have felt the call of PVP combat and left our ranks to find notoriety among the stars

Fleet Operations Division (retired)

  • SCFS Distinguished Combat Medal

  • SCFS Distinguished Command Medal

  • SCFS Distinguished Logistics Medal

  • SCFS Medal of Cooperation

  • SCFS Medal of Leadership

  • SCFS Medal of Scouting

  • Spiked Signal

  • Vulfpup Graduate

SCFS Distinguished Combat Medal - Sustained superior combat performance while under threat of physical attack or harassment
SCFS Distinguished Command Medal - Sustained superior command performance while under threat of physical attack or harassment.
SCFS Distinguished Logistics Medal - Sustained superior logistics performance while under threat of physical attack or harassment
SCFS Medal of Cooperation - Awarded to Signaleers willing to tackle New Eden's most challenging group content.
SCFS Medal of Leadership - Awarded to Signaleers willing to carry the heavy responsibility of fleet leadership on their shoulders.
SCFS Medal of Scouting - Awarded to Signaleers willing to light a way for their fleet and protect them from the shadows.
Spiked Signal - Awarded to pilots placing in the top three during Signal Cartel's in-house PVP tournament on the Singularity server.
Vulfpup Graduate - Awarded to pilots for successful completion of a Vulfpup fleet, thereby demonstrating that they are ready to fly with the big dogs.
