Crikey Freight is a hauling, mining, and industry corporation whose membership is made up solely of Signal Cartel alts. As such, it is immune to wardecs.

What We Do[edit]

Crikey Freight has regular mining fleets and members undertake all forms of industry including:

  • Gas huffing
  • Hauling
  • Manufacture
  • Planetary Production
  • Trading
  • Mining

We all support each other and embrace the same friendly and helpful ethos of Signal Cartel.

Services to Signal Cartel[edit]

  • Crikey Freight provides low-cost hauling for Signal Cartel members.


Note: A jump freighter service is also available for a higher fee.

  • Crikey Freight also administers the New Member Relocation Service (SCRAMS) and contributes to the Blueprint Buyback Service (SCRUBS) too.

Crikey Freight Wants You(r Alt)![edit]

While hauling with your Signal Cartel character is not advised due to frequent wardecs, there is an alternative. Crikey Freight is immune to wardecs and is actively recruiting hauling and industry alts of Signal Cartel members.

So is this just another corp in the EvE-Scout Enclave Alliance?[edit]

No! We are an entirely separate corp, but we have Signal Cartel Leadership approval to recruit alts of Signal Cartel members. While we provide a lot of support to Signal Cartel, we only use information about Signal Cartel and its members that is publicly available.

What are the rules?[edit]

  • First and foremost, we are close enough linked to Signal Cartel that we cannot risk the reputation of Crikey Freight having a negative impact on the reputation of Signal Cartel. To this end, members of Crikey Freight are bound by the same Credo as Signal Cartel. You are only allowed to defend yourself and other members of your own fleet. No aggression.
  • If you leave Signal Cartel, then your alt must leave Crikey Freight too.
  • Information gained by your Signal Cartel character may only be used by your Crikey Freight alt for SC-sanctioned activities.

What are the requirements to join?[edit]

Your Signal Cartel character must be a C1 member or higher. Click here for more information regarding Signal Cartel membership levels and how to request a title upgrade. Ultimately, Crikey Freight membership decisions are at the discretion of leadership.

Are there minimum requirements to remain in Crikey Freight?[edit]

No, if you are still in Signal Cartel then you may stay in Crikey. In some circumstances, long-term inactive members may be removed but can rejoin again any time.

Where are you based?[edit]

Crikey Freight is headquartered in:

  • Solar System: Zoohen
Station: Zoohen VII - Core Complexion Inc. Factory

However, we have many offices in New Eden, strategically located to give members access to all the services we need to carry out our activities.

How do I join?[edit]

  • Simply apply with your alt to Crikey Freight in-game. Please put the name of your Signal Cartel character in the application information.
  • Please contact us through Discord for more information.

What are the benefits to members?[edit]

  • Help, support, and learning
  • Friendship!
  • Free stuff! Loads of hulls, modules, consumables, drones, PI Command centres and more!

We encourage and embrace team work as an important and effective way to overcome the issues presented to industrialists in the face of ongoing resource scarcity.
