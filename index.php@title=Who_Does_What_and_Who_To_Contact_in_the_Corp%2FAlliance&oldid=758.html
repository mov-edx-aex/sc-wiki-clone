Who to Contact

The Signal Cartel and EvE-Scout leadership team are available to you! If you have a Credo matter, an area of concern within the corporation or alliance, a big idea for some significant change in how we operate or what we do, or any other question or issue that you consider in need of leadership attention, it is entirely appropriate to approach leadership with it directly. In such cases, please feel free to reach out to us as specified below. We are accessible, approachable, and want to give your situation the serious consideration it deserves.

Many corporations in EVE Online of our size (and even smaller) forbid line members from approaching leadership directly, and instead require you to work through layers of bureaucracy in order to have your voice heard. In fact, contacting leadership directly in some corps will get you kicked!

We never want to be like that in Signal Cartel. Nonetheless, the leadership team is comprised of mere mortals with full and busy lives, so we can only be available to a reasonable and manageable extent. Therefore, please do not contact leadership frivolously or for matters that can be addressed just as well (and perhaps even better) by other corp members. For example, don’t approach leadership directly with questions about ship fits or general advice on how to play the game. That is what Alliance chat and the forums are for.

Credo & Signal Cartel The CEO of Signal Cartel should be your first point of contact for Credo issues and anything relating to Signal Cartel as a whole. This is currently Thrice Hapus. He checks in-game email once each day and is readily available on Discord for issues that are more time sensitive. If there is a deliverable attached to your request, sending Thrice an email is the best way to make sure it happens. He is US-ET.

EvE-Scout, Thera scanning, and Forums All questions about the Thera scanning program, eve-scout.com, and the EvE-Scout Enclave alliance as a whole should be directed to Johnny Splunk. He also maintains the corp forums at https://forums.eve-scout.com. Johnny prefers contact to be initiated via Discord. He is US-PT.

Diplomacy Please refer any and all diplomatic incidents to any or all members of the leadership team.

Divisional Issues Please address division-specific matters to the appropriate divisional head, as listed above.
