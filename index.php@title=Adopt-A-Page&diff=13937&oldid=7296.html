Welcome to the Exploration Adopt-A-Page Initiative

The Explorer's Compendium Needs You!

We have a goal of making The Explorer’s Compendium the best exploration resource in the Universe, but we need your help to make this dream come true.

What is Adopt-A-Page?[edit]

  • The Exploration Adopt-A-Page Initiative is modeled after the real life Adopt-A-Highway litter control program, in which volunteers pledge to keep a section of road free from litter. In exchange for this service, the individual or group has their name displayed on the particular section of road they maintain.
  • In this same manner, Signaleers can "adopt" an exploration page on our wiki.
  • By adopting a page, Signaleers pledge to edit the page and build it out to completion within 3 months from the adoption date.
  • In exchange, the newly minted wiki editor will get their name and picture in a banner at the top of their adopted page for a 3-month period.
  • After 3 months, assuming the page is properly fleshed out, the wiki editor that adopted the page will then receive recognition at the bottom of their page - forever acknowledging their awesome contribution to our shared knowledge!
  • It's our hope that this initiative will spark further interest in the wiki and therefore our ability to sustain continued growth. The ultimate goal is to make The Explorer’s Compendium the best exploration resource in the Universe!

How to Edit the Wiki[edit]

  • The skills needed to edit a wiki page are pretty minimal - if you can read and type, you are good to go.
  • Please read and familiarize yourself with our Wiki Editing Guidelines.
  • A basic understanding of Wiki Markup is helpful.
  • This Cheatsheet is useful to have on hand.
  • Also, please cite your sources and give credit where credit is due - please no plagiarizing! Click here for more information about using references.

What If I'm a New Player or Not an Exploration Expert (yet)?[edit]

That's completely fine! Please still sign up and participate! This is meant to be fun and will also be a great learning experience.

  • Some suggestions:
    • Pick the brains of our older (I mean, veteran) Signaleers. This will give you an excuse to chat them up and get to know them! Signaleers are a friendly bunch and always willing to share what we know!
    • Dig through the old EvE-Scout Forums and our Discord Server (there is a wealth of information just waiting to be uncovered)
    • Check out the Educational Resource Archive
    • Also, don't be shy about using outside sources, such those contained in:
      • The Explorer's Addendum

How to Adopt-A-Page[edit]

  1. Select a page to adopt from the table below.
  2. Change the appropriate Status column from "Available" to "Adopted".
  3. Type your name into the appropriate Editor column (replacing "Your Name Here").
  4. Change the appropriate Adopted Until column from "3 months from now" to the correct month/year (by adding 3 months).
  5. Starting editing YOUR page, ask questions, and HAVE FUN!
  6. After you finish editing a page and are completely done to the best of your ability, please change the Status column from "Adopted" to "Completed".

Note: Each editor can adopt only one page at a time. However, once you finish a page - please feel free to adopt another!
