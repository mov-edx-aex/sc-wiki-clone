Signal Cartel Relocation and Moving Service (SCRAMS)

  • Free home station relocation service for new members

Created and managed by: Xavec

Contents

  • 1 What is It?
  • 2 How Do I Take Advantage?
  • 3 How Do I Make a Contract?
  • 4 The Small Print
  • 5 Reference

What is It?

  • A free relocation service offered to New Signaleer Recruits who have been in the corp less than 60 days, including returning members.
  • This service will relocate the belongings of new Signaleers from their current high-sec home station to a Signal Cartel high-sec station of their choice - most likely Zoohen, Saisio, or Gelhan. Relocating to our HQ station in Thera may be considered on a case-by-case basis.
Signal Cartel Corporate Offices
* Zoohen III – Theology Council Tribunal
* Gelhan V – Moon 10 – DED Logistic Support
* Saisio VIII – Moon 4 – Perkone Factory
* Thera XII - The Sanctuary Institute of Paleocybernetics (Wormhole Space - SCRAMS hauls usually declined)
  • It is hoped that this service will help with recruitment and retention of brand new Signaleers and help them get as much of their stuff out of their old stations and trade hubs as safely as possible!
  • We will also relocate your belongings from NPC Nullsec or Lowsec NPC stations only. You will need to pay for the jump and cyno fuel - price will be agreed in advance.
  • This service has been approved and is overseen by Aldar Roanaok, our CEO.

How Do I Take Advantage?

Important: Signaleers who wish to take advantage of the service should message Xavec via EVEmail detailing:

  • The volume of cargo
  • Approximate value of the cargo
  • The start and end stations.

Ideally all ships should be repackaged, however you will lose any rigs by repackaging so don't repackage ships with expensive rigs.

Unexpected SCRAMS contracts will be declined.

Any questions should be directed to Xavec in the EvE-Scout Discord Server or you can ping @admin in the Crikey Freight Discord Server.

Once approved by Xavec you can create a courier contract (see next section).

How Do I Make a Contract?

  • Please go to the Contracts page to learn how to create a contract.
  • SCRAMS specific instructions are located at the bottom of the contracts page.

The Small Print

  • The time period of eligibility is 60 days after joining the corp – new corp members must initiate the move service within that time. An initial evemail to Xavec counts as initiating the move service.
  • The request for a free move may be for one move only, however the contract may be split to accommodate volume or collateral limitations. Gathering belongings from more than one station may be considered at Xavec’s discretion.
  • The pilot will be instructed to create a private Courier Contract to Crikey Freight. The collateral should be maximum 110% of the value of the cargo and the reward set to zero. The haul contract ensures there is a record of the deal.
  • The maximum collateral per haul contract is 2 billion ISK unless by prior agreement.
  • Unless otherwise agreed, the maximum volume per haul contract is 500,000m3.
  • Haul contracts may not have any assembled containers inside unless by prior specific agreement with Xavec
  • Moves will not be made to or from player-owned structures unless agreed with Xavec.
  • The time limit on accepting hauling contracts should be set to 2 weeks. The time limit to complete should be set to 14 days. This is not an express service.
  • Moves must be to one of the SC High-sec offices.
  • Free hauls are Hi-sec only excluding hisec islands.
  • Low-sec hauls, Hauls from Hisec Islands and hauls from NPC Nullsec are available but we reserve the right to charge a fee. This is to cover the cost of Jump Freighter fuel only and no profit will be made.
  • Sovereign Nullsec and J-space hauls (except Thera) will not be considered under any circumstances.
  • Crikey Freight alts are bound by the Credo.
