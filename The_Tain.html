TRAVEL NOTE:
DUO-51 is deep in 0.0 space, involving jumps across a number of different alliance territories, many of which may be operating under “Not Blue, Shoot It” protocol. Gate camps (including warp interdiction bubbles) can be found often in transitioning from 0.0 to high security space, as well as on other gates. Caution is advised.

TripTik044 Expedition Highlights

Visit a sight in DUO-51, where law enforcement uses a criminal's opulent residence as a base.

Introduction

At times, law enforcement strategically employs the opulent residence of a high-profile criminal as their operational base to track down a specific target. Presently, such tactics unfold in DUO-51. Martokar Alash, aligned with the Angel Cartel, wielded substantial wealth and influence, enabling him to exert control over an entire Minmatar Industry Station. However, such a formidable presence necessitates removal, given the requisite political connections with the Thukker Tribe.

Supervising the operation are two Nidhoggur-class Carriers and three Tempest-class Battleships. The aftermath paints a vivid picture of destruction. A once lively station, now stripped of its defenses and lying dormant, bows sadly amidst a field of debris, frozen corpses, and the remnants of ghost ships.

The Republic's determination to neutralize this warlord is evident, with its agents actively recruiting on-site for the impending mission.
