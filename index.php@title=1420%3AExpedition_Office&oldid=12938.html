TripTik047 Expedition Highlights

Visit a once-imposing Gallente station, now scorched and silent.

Introduction

In the eerie aftermath of Thera's enigmatic calamity, a once-grand Gallente station stands scarred and silent, its mysteries deepening amidst the remnants of destruction.

Locations

  • Thera

In the whispering void of Thera, a once-majestic station stands as a spectral relic, its fate veiled in an enigmatic shroud. Scarred and scorched, this Gallente-designed structure near Thera VII reveals a profound destruction. Clouds of plasma, gas, and dust cloak the station, its interior gutted by fire and explosions, leaving behind an empty husk.

Adjacent to the devastation, an untouched satellite, seemingly unscathed amid chaos, adds a layer of mystery. Once a radiation monitoring device, it assumed the role of an emergency communications relay. Yet, precise energy weapon strikes silenced its processing cores, leaving questions hanging in the cosmic void.

The wreckage paints a silent tableau—delicate structures survive where others crumble. Was this relay erected post-calamity, necessitating emergency communications from unknown sources? The unanswered riddles intensify as we ponder who wielded such precise energy weapons. Were the Sisters protecting their secrets, or does a darker player lurk in the shadows of Thera's mystery? The Matyrhan Lakat-Hro supercarrier's scorched interiors hold no answers, only deepening the cosmic enigma that envelopes this forsaken station.
