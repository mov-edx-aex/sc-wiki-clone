In-Game Activities

Tend Wormhole Rescue Caches and Rescue Stranded Pilots with EvE-Scout Rescue (ESR)

EvE-Scout Rescue (ESR) is New Eden's premier wormhole rescue service and is one of the oldest divisions in Signal Cartel. ESR consists of two programs:

  • EvE-Scout Rescue Cache (ESRC)
    • Provides a basic emergency resource kit for capsuleers stranded in wormholes, regardless of alliance, sovereignty, or playstyle.
    • ESRC was previously discussed in the Orientation Guide - First Week.
  • Search and Rescue (SAR)
    • The SAR program is specifically organized to rescue capsuleers stranded in wormhole systems where there is no ESRC present, or in situations when a stranded capsuleer is unable to utilize the equipment in an existing rescue cache.

Scan and Map Thera with Our Thera Scanning Endeavor

  • First, what is Thera? According to Mynxee, Signal Cartel Co-founder, "Thera is a wormhole system akin to Grand Central station...on average, 20-some wormhole connections spawn there daily that go to locations all over New Eden: high-sec, low-sec, null-sec, and other wormholes. People--especially explorers, but also many PvP'ers--use Thera as a convenient shortcut to move quickly around New Eden. You may wish to visit Thera for use as a travel hub, to see the sights there, or to participate in EvE-Scout's Thera Scanning Endeavor."
  • EvE-Scout, our sister corporation, operates the Thera Scanning Endeavor as a crowdsourced service tasked with scanning and recording the current wormhole access locations into and out of the hub system of Thera for the benefit of all of New Eden. The wormholes are exclusively scouted by members of Signal Cartel. This is the original project that lead to the creation of EvE-Scout and Signal Cartel! Thera scouts are also compensated by the generous donations of capsuleers that find our service invaluable.
  • Additionally, Tekufah, a fellow Signaleer, will generously donate a fitted Covert Ops ship to you if you try out Thera Scanning for the first time! Click here for details!

Track Dangerous Storms Across New Eden with Our Signal Cartel Storm Tracking Service

Nullsec is currently experiencing wandering storms that slowly travel between systems called Metaliminal Storms. These weather events produce a variety of environmental effects that impact all ships brave enough to enter them. Help us report the stormy weather for the benefit of all capsuleers!

  • Click here to learn how to track storms with us!

Work Towards Joining the Anoikis Division (AD) and LIVE in a Wormhole!

Jumping through your first wormhole can be a scary proposition. Exploring the unknown of J-space can be even scarier. Permanently living there?! You must be crazy! Well, not for our brave members of the Anoikis Division (AD) that elevate wormhole living to an art form. Living in a wormhole provides its own unique set of challenges - constantly changing ingress/egress routes, sporadic access to trade hubs and known-space jump clones, and constant danger from PvP-focused pilots and corps. The rewards, other than ISK, are vast - thrills, excitement, camaraderie, and just plain fun! It's Wormhole Living at its Finest!

Here is a partial list of activities that AD members can pursue:

  • The explorer‚Äôs mainstay of scanning and hacking of data/relic sites.
  • PvE Combat: Sleeper sites and other NPCs in our wormhole, as well as other systems accessed through connecting wormholes.
  • Planetary Interaction (PI): A good way to generate a passive income. Our wormhole has several planets and can produce, up to and including, P4 products.
  • Mining: Random spawns of ore, ice fields and gas clouds within and outside of our wormhole. We also have a moondrill for extraction of moon ore for mining.
  • Industrial: Manufacturing and reprocessing. Other industrial services, such as Invention, Reactions, are available.
  • Participation in the Corp‚Äôs Search and Rescue (SAR) and EvE-Scout Rescue Cache (ESRC) programs.

If interested, Signaleers must meet the AD's eligibility requirements. Therefore, while you can't join the AD within your first month, you can start your journey towards living the wormhole life now! Click here for more info on the AD and also visit the AD forum for further info and discussion.
