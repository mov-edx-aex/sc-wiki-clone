What Is The Thera Scan Tool[edit]

Mapping Thera as part of the EvE-Scout Thera Scanning Endeavor is a precise activity â€“ that Signaleers sometimes have to do while under duress. The Thera Scan Tool is a web-based program that helps to simplify and speed up the process of scanning and recording Thera signatures, while reducing typing errors. The tool makes it quick to see what signatures and bookmarks are expired or new, then makes updating them easier and more consistent. Ultimately, it helps new pilots learn to Thera Scan well and seasoned ones to do it faster.

The Thera Scan Tool is available here: https://therascan.info/

How Do I Use It?[edit]

  • To learn how to use the Thera Scan Tool, please read our Thera Scanning Quick Start Guide.
