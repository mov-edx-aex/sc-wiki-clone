NOTE:  This page contains potentially outdated information and has been preserved for historical use only as part of the Signal Cartel Archive.

Credo fireside chats are discussions that demonstrate how members individually interpret and apply the Credo in response to various situations and challenges in New Eden. They are intended to provide food for thought about your own interpretation of the Credo.

Please bear in mind that opinions about Credo application may evolve over time.

Members are encouraged to conduct their own Credo Fireside Chats as a service to the corp as well as the general public. Just choose 3-5 Credo-related questions, select your panel of participants, and schedule a date. During the chat, facilitate and record, then edit the audio, upload the audio somewhere, and post a link and summary of what was discussed in the Credo and Culture Discussion section of the forums. The Leadership Team will then decide whether to publicly share your Credo Fireside Chat with the wider EVE community.

Credo Fireside Chat #1 (Click here to listen)[edit]

Published 8 Aug 2015

Participants: Johnny Splunk (Signal Cartel Co-founder, Leadership Team), Mynxee (Signal Cartel Co-founder, Former CEO), Helios Anduath (former Director), G8keeper (Signal Cartel Co-founder, audio editor)

  • Is consensual PvP permitted using the Duel mechanic?
  • Can we shoot abandoned things? (e.g. Bubbles, Depots, MTUs, Ships)
  • Is taking loot from SovNull or occupied wormholes an act of aggression and against the Credo?
  • What if my actions disproportionately benefit one side of a conflict? (e.g. providing scouting Info)
  • Is contesting a site considered aggression?
  • Can I participate in Live Events? What about NPSI public roams?
