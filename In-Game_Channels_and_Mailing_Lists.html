This is a list of channels owned and run by either the leadership team or corpmates for various purposes. Some are public access, some require you to be a member of a specific program or division (the Anoikis Division, for example).

Leadership discourages the formation of channels for "general" chat among SC members as a replacement for our admittedly busy Alliance chat. This helps maintain a sense of cohesion as a corp, fosters better awareness of everything we have going on, and helps ensure that more experienced players will be in the mix when newer players need their help and advice.

However, if you see the need for a channel to serve a particular need or support a particular program, by all means create it. Leadership request the courtesy of being made operators in channels for Signal Cartel programs or member initiatives that serve our membership.
If you want a channel added to the list below please contact them with the following info:

  • Channel Name
  • Owner (who is in charge of the channel)
  • Access (who's allowed in there)
  • Purpose (brief description)
