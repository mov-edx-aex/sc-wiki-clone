"Logistics refers to ships and modules that provide remote support to other ships. In real life, the term "logistics" is typically used to describe the coordination of military troops and supplies, and EVE University has a Logistics Division that serves this role."
E-Uni

If you think this might be your niche, then below you will find a few fits with training requirements for your progression in the logi role. Keep in mind that these are all armour rep fits. These fits will also help you to reach a level where you are able to run Vulfpeck Fleets and make some serious ISK to pay for the hulls and fittings. If you want to try logi to see if you like it without having to buy and fit your own ship, then the Augoror fit is the one to train for. If you find that you enjoy the role, then keep going and train for the ships after it!

Conclusion[edit]

So what's next? Well, you may have noticed that not all of your skills above are at level 5 yet; why don't you start maxing those out to completely maximize your abilities and reps? I would start with the logistic skills. Then it's up to you; maybe you want to see what shield repping is like, then go for it and find a shield fleet. The sky is the limit from here!


Please direct any questions or concerns to Katherine Skysong or Xavec using in-game mail, discord or the forums.
