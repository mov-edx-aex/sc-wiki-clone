TIP:
Set each system in the TripTik as a waypoint, then choose Autopilot options to establish a route for your expedition.
TRAVEL NOTE:
Waypoints are located in relatively quiet lowsec systems. However, be mindful when in these systems of opportunistic capsuleers who may seek to free you from your ship and pod. When you reach the System warp to the beacon.

TripTik046 Expedition Highlights

Traverse the stars and celebrate the spirit of discovery.

Introduction

Mark726 Travel Agency is your premier destination for efficient and secure interstellar travel services. Specializing in jump route planning, the agency ensures pilots navigate New Eden's vast expanse with ease.

Locations

  • Mozzidit

Embark on epic voyages with confidence, guided by the legacy of Mark726, a revered pioneer in EVE Online's navigation community. Mark726 Travel Agency upholds his commitment to excellence, offering not just route planning, but a homage to a visionary who shaped the way pilots traverse the cosmos.

”Never stop because you are afraid - you are never so likely to be wrong.” - Mark726
