Greetings Astrophotographers!

So, what is the Observatory? It is fair to say the majority of, perhaps even all, explorers share a common love for the beauty that New Eden has to offer. As we travel the stars, we often try to capture and share the wonders we find along the way with our fellow capsuleers via the images that we take. We post and share these moments in time in many different ways on many different sites. There’s no one place to go, no observatory, no gallery that showcases the marvels of our cluster. It's our hope that the EvE-Scout Observatory will be that common place folks go to for sharing and seeing images from around the cluster.

Please post your favorite images directly to our #eve-observatory channel on the EvE-Scout Enclave Discord server. The Observatory looks forward to seeing your submissions!

  • For screenshot tips see Katia Sae's blog post: “Astrophotography 101”.
    • Pro tip: Ctrl-F9 hides the UI. The “Print Screen” button takes the screenshot.
  • For inspiration check out Razorien's (now CCP Aperture) Flickr album located here.

Note: Signal Cartel no longer use Flickr for our Observatory but the photos are still browsable for archival purposes.
