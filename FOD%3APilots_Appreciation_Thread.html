The Fleet Ops Division (FOD) would not exist (nor be any fun) without fearless pilots volunteering their time and talents to our Division. The FOD Leadership Team recognizes these fleet-related feats by awarding several different in-game medals (and a few special, secret ones!). We wish to thank the following Signaleers for their dedication and service both past and present:
