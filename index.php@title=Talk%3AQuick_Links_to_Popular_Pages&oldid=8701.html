Method for link selection[edit]

Okay, this being Eve, I made a spreadsheet.

The spreadsheet was a list of all URL references to the Wiki from our Discord over the last 9 months (23 Aug 2022 - 23 May 2023), generally filtering out any appearing just in #sc-wiki (since that is biased towards development of new pages). The raw data is given below. Wiki pages with two or more links are included in the Quick Links page. Two additional links were added, one pointing to the Credo and a second pointing to an index for the Explorer's Handbook.

We intend to update this page every six months, by searching again for new repeated references to the Wiki from Discord.
