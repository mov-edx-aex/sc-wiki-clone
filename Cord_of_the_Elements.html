WAYPOINT SYSTEMS
(Alphabetical Order)
  • D-B7YK
TIP:

The location is in nullsec, so a covops-capable ship is a good choice.

The Cord of the Elements is part of the system's skybox and visible from anywhere in it. The Retreat Center is marked by a warpable beacon.
TRAVEL NOTE:
D-B7YK is located in a fairly quiet area of space, in a relatively isolated constellation in the Syndicate region. It's accessible via wormhole, needlejack filament, or nearby Gallente hisec. This part of Syndicate isn't very busy, and the route in is unlikely to be camped.

TripTik027 Expedition Highlights

Visit a system where you can view the Cord of the Elements and the Intaki Retreat Center.

Introduction

Less than a dozen jumps from the Intaki Syndicate's home system of Poitot, the Executive Retreat Center provides a rare and breathtaking view of an otherwise concealed dark cloud anomaly, and a markedly less breathtaking view of an admittedly luxurious facility probably used by bored Syndicate executives for mandatory team-building exercises. The Cord is visible from anywhere in D-B7YK, but the Retreat Center is a natural choice for screenshots. This is a pretty new landmark, only added to the game in April 2021.
