Cargo Sorter is a tool created by Mzsbi Haev and designed to calculate the most valuable combination of loot by mass and value that fits within the cargo hold of your ship. Min-Maxing your loot has never been easier!

The tool can be found here: https://therascan.info/cargo/

Contact Mzsbi Haev in-game or in the forums here with bugs or suggestions.
