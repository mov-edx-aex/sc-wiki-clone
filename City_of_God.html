TripTik016 Expedition Highlights

The ruins of an ancient Amarrian city.

Introduction

Built by the megalomaniac emperor Zaragram II some 2000 years ago, intended to be the home of the living god, namely himself. Zaragram set out to uproot and eradicate many of the most sacred traditions of the Amarrians, replacing them by his own mad ideas on divinity. He called his city Mezagorm, meaning Vision of God.

Things came about differently than Zaragram wished. Just when the construction of his glorious city, located in the system of Shastal, was completed, emperor Zaragram was assassinated by his grandson and his city laid to waste. Later emperors, under pressure from the clergy, did their utmost to bury his memory.

Locations

  • Shastal
On entering the system, look for the City of God landmark and warp to it. The site sits above a lava planet.
