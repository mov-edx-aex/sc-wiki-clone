WAYPOINT SYSTEMS
(Alphabetical Order)
  • ZH3-BS
TIP:
Set each system in the TripTik as a waypoint, then choose Autopilot options to establish a route for your expedition.
TRAVEL NOTE:
System is accessible via wormhole, needlejack filament, or through many jumps across different alliance territories. The route is likely to be camped. When in the system warp to the beacon.

TripTik051 Expedition Highlights

Explore a covert narcotics manufacturing facility, steeped in secrecy and intrigue.

Introduction

In the System of ZH3-BS Lies an Outpost that Also, serves as a Caldari Cosmos Mission with a Host of Different Agents.

Locations

  • ZH3-BS

In the system of ZH3-BS lies Settlers Waypoint where an ethereal outpost flourishes, conceived by elusive gas harvesters entwined in conflicts over high-stakes negotiations. Beneath the ever-watchful eye of a formidable Phoenix-class dreadnought, the compound pulsates with an otherworldly energy, veiled in mystery from clandestine narcotics manufacturing.
